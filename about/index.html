Info / About

The Juice Dispatch is a new civic news program in Whitehorse that is part of the Local Journalism Initiative. CJUC 92.5 FM has been able to hire two journalists for this project. The program will focus on in-depth reporting of civic topics in Whitehorse - housing and development, accessibility, garbage and waste..

Shows will be broadcast on the radio Sundays and Wednesdays at 1pm and will also be available wherever you listen to podcasts.

Community participation in this project is key! We want to hear from you - story ideas, tips, special guests. Do you want to be involved in how stories are told?

We have a mandate to involve community members in story production and presentation. Let us know! We're at or by direct message on @thejuicedispatch.
